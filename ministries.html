Our Ministries

Hello! Welcome to Music & Dance Ministry!


If you have a passion for music either you're a musician, singer, dancer and want to use your talent for the Lord, We are inviting you to come and join us! Together let us lift the name of Jesus and serve Him through Praise and Worship.

If you are interested, join us every Saturday, 6pm at In Jesus Name Church Leona Subdivision, Balut, Orani, Bataan.
