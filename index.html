About

In Jesus Name Church


Loving, Caring, Worshipping,

Christ centered church

We are a community of believers who are committed to loving God and loving others. Our church is centered around the teachings of Jesus Christ, and we seek to follow His example in all that we do.

We are a welcoming and inclusive community, and we invite you to come and see what we're all about. Whether you're looking for a place to worship, a community to belong to, or a way to make a difference in the world, we believe that you'll find all of that and more at our loving, caring, worshipping Christ-centered church.
